Enemies

Enemies are hostile creatures in Cones in Space. They generally cannot move upwards or downwards, save for flying enemies. All enemies typically drop woodchips. However, some enemies drop different resources.

Bosses

Bosses are hostile creatures in cones in space. They usually drop items important to progression.

Ways to Spawn Bosses:

  • Coneiverse: spawn them by placing a radio on their spawning circle

  • nexus: spawn them by placing a tomb on their spawning circle

  • buff giga green man: spawn by placing sugon's bones on its summoning circle

  • larptic bosses: spawn by placing a larptic soul on their summoning circles

  • secret boss: spawn by placing a cone plush on its summoning circle

  • dungeons: already spawned

  • szar: spawns on a meteor belt day 75, can also be spawned via szar shrine
