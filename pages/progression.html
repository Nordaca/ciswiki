Progression Guide

Everything ya gotta know, and we mean everything, baby



Cones In Space is a long game, with lots to do. This guide will give you the necessary steps on how to beat the game.

    ACT I

  1. Craft a Rocket (2 Steel)

  2. Defeat the Bosses on Orange Heck and MB-2

  3. Clear the Grass Dungeon on Flesharia

  4. Craft the Tier 2 Rocket, then collect Sugon's Bones (Found on Tealia)

  5. Defeat the bosses on Magmantic and MB-3

  6. Defeat Super Octobro on Aquatia

  7. Craft the Anti-Mucus to walk through transparent green blocks.

  8. Beat the Sewer Dungeon on Spookia

  9. Craft the Thermal Rocket

  10. Defeat the bosses on Shady Gray and Shiveria

  11. Defeat Icen Hero on MB-5 and Stone Cone on Powderia

  12. Craft the Anti-Wave so you can walk through transparent blue blocks

  13. Clear the Abyss on Snuskipator

  14. Craft the Wormhole Rocket

    15. ACT II

  15. Enter WH-1

  16. Collect Emeralds on one of the two Emerald planets

  17. Go to Emeraldia and defeat the Anarchist

  18. Go to the Nexus Gate and place the Red Ruby you got from the Anarchist onto the pedestal on the planet

  19. Defeat the bosses on Parking Lot, Shower Room, and Nexus Hub

  20. Exit Nexus, Return to the Coneiverse

  21. Place emeralds onto a green pedestal found on every planet (Save for the Nexus planets and Nexus Gate)

  22. Go to WH-2

  23. Spawn the Buff Giga Green Man by placing Sugon's Bones on the Buff Giga Green Man's summoning circle

  24. Defeat the Buff Giga Green Man and collect the Serum.

  25. Defeat the Larptic Tissue

  26. Craft the Larptic Cure

  27. Survive the Larptic Season on Borgulon so Yellow Cone saves you!

    28. ACT III

  28. Obtain the "Yellow Cone Pad". You can now walk through yellow transparent blocks.

  29. Clear the Tomb Dungeon on Ashpit

  30. Clear the Fire Dungeon on Orange Heck

  31. Buy one of Yellow Cone's ship parts from the green man professor on Flesharia.

  32. Talk to the Larptic Core on one of the two Larptic Planets. He will give you one of Yellow Cone's ship pieces!

  33. Return to Borgulon with all the ship parts and enter Yellow Cone's rocket.

  34. Defeat the Larptic Core

    35. F.I.N.
