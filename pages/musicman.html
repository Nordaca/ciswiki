Music Man

Developer

Tiernan Brazil, or "Music Man", is the lead musician on a_coneish_one's projects since Cones in Space (2023). He was brought on version 2.x to orchestrate the soundtrack for the planets and other things, like dungeons. He is a solo-developer who enjoys programming with GameMaker Studio: 2 in his spare time. He has a bandcamp (Cascade) where he posts his original music.
