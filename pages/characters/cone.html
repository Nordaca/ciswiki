Cone

Character


"Guess we'll go right back to being CONES" -Cone, replying to Yellow Cone when being asked "What're we gonna do now?"



Cone is the protagonist in Cones in Space, he is a standard cone civilian who works at a company called "Cone Space Flyers Inc." A space exploration company that specializes in reasearching flora and fauna.

Cone is a fairly average member of his species, his crowning achievements are:

  • discovering a multitude of planets, flora, and fauna

  • graduated with the 11th best marks in the class he took for spaceship flying in college (still crashed)

  • he once beat his nephew in checkers

  • is the employee at cone space flyers inc with the most uses of the built in spaceship respawn machine, due to his large number of deaths

  • has invented the cone of life due to his frequent deaths

Prior to Cones in Space, Cone does fairly normal missions with his partner Yellow Cone, recieving missions from their boss, Papa Corporal Cone

After Cones in Space, cone is awarded the cone medical award for creating the larptic cure (definitely did not steal it from a previous civilization) and becomes a high ranking member of the newly formed Larptic Extinction Corps (L.E.C.).
