Planets

Full List as of 2.5.1


Planets are large areas you can explore in Cones in Space. Each planet has useful fauna, and maybe even bosses or dungeons!

Meteor Belts

Meteor Belts are bits of meteors that are floating in space, orbiting around planets. Some meteor belts may even have bosses on them!

Moons

Moons are smaller planets that orbit around normal planets, some have bosses, or even hidden secrets!

Oceans

Every planet and Moon has an infinite ocean. The oceans house useful materials you can find on randomly generated islands. Some hazards can generate in oceans as well, so be careful!
