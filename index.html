Controls:

  • W, A, S, D to move

  • Space to Jump, or skip text

  • Shift to use shield

  • Shift + Hammer Equipped to break objects without destroying floors

  • Shift + Hammer + No Objects in reticle = only destroying floors

  • Shift + Place to place a floor without a roof

  • Q to ove the cursor onto your exact position

  • LMB to use item/select

  • Scroll Wheel to change what you're holding

  • G + Yellow Cone Pad to tell Yellow Cone to go

  • H + Yellow Cone Pad to tell Yellow Cone to stop

  • Z, X, C to Use Vehicle

  • E to Exit Vehicle
